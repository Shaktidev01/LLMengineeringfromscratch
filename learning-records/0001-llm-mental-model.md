## What was taught
Lesson 1: What Is an LLM?

## Key insights
- LLMs are next-word prediction machines, not databases or rule engines
- "Intelligence" emerges from pattern-matching across hundreds of billions of words
- RTX 5060 (8GB VRAM) runs 7B models; 70B+ requires cloud
- Product builder's role: wrap the LLM, not build it
